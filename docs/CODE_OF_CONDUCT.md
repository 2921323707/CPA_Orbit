# Code of Conduct

CPA Orbit welcomes contributors regardless of background or experience.

Be respectful, specific, and constructive. Critique ideas and code rather than people. Do not harass, threaten, expose private information, or publish credentials and account data. Maintainers may edit or remove content and restrict participation when behavior puts contributors or users at risk.

When reporting conduct concerns, avoid public escalation that could reveal personal or security-sensitive information. A private maintainer contact will be added with the public repository.
